A different shape of email AI

6 features

Most AI mail clients lock you into one vendor and charge a monthly subscription. Mail Digest is open source: pick Claude, GPT, or Gemini, bring your own API key, and pay the provider directly.

Premium models

Claude, GPT, and Gemini built in. Choose the model that fits your quality bar and budget — Sonnet, GPT‑4.1, Gemini Flash, or whatever you configure.

Your keys, your provider

API keys stay on your machine. Requests go from your desktop straight to Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google — no Mail Digest server in the middle.

Privacy by design

Gmail OAuth tokens live in your OS keychain. Email goes from Google to your machine, then to the AI provider you chose. No Mail Digest backend, no analytics, no telemetry.

Daily brief, automatic

Set the hour. Wake up to your inbox already triaged: priority, action items, calendar events, suggested replies — ready to skim with your first coffee.

Editorial reading experience

Designed to read like a morning paper, not a notification feed. Color-coded sections, calm palette, instant scanning.

Open and inspectable

MIT licensed. Audit the prompts. Audit the network calls. Fork it. Build the integrations you actually need — not the ones a vendor decides to ship.

Three steps. About five minutes the first time.

01

Connect Gmail

Create your own OAuth credentials in Google Cloud Console — takes about three minutes. Mail Digest never sees them; they live on your machine.

02

Add your API key

Choose Claude, GPT, or Gemini and paste your API key. Switch provider or model any time without changing your workflow.

03

Hit refresh

The daily brief renders in seconds. Set a time and the app prepares it for you every morning.
